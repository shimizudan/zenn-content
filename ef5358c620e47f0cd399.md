# $$\int{}{}\frac{2x+1}{x^2+x+1}dx$$

$$\begin{aligned}&
\int{}{}\frac{2x+1}{x^2+x+1}dx\\
&=\int{}{}\frac{(x^2+x+1)'}{x^2+x+1}dx\\
&=\log\big|x^2+x+1\big|+C\\
&=\color{blue}{\log(x^2+x+1)+C}\\
&\qquad\quad (\because x^2+x+1>0)
\end{aligned}$$
